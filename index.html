Frequently Asked Questions

Is this chatbot a replacement for a therapist?

No. While this IB chatbot offers emotional support and stress management tips, it is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are in crisis, please contact a school counselor or a licensed therapist.

How does the IB stress support chatbot work?

The chatbot uses a large language model trained to provide helpful and encouraging responses based on a vast dataset of information. It's designed to simulate a supportive conversation with a knowledgeable peer.

Is my conversation private?

Yes. I prioritize your privacy. Conversations are not stored or logged. Please review the full disclaimer for more details on data handling.

What kind of help for IB students can I get?

You can ask for study tips, advice on managing deadlines, strategies for coping with exam anxiety, ideas for CAS projects, and general encouragement to navigate the challenges of the IB program.
